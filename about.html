About AI Resource Hub

Making AI more enjoyable, easier, and more understandable for everyone.

Why this exists

AI moves fast, and it's easy to feel behind. New tools launch constantly, terminology changes, and knowing which tool to use — let alone how to prompt it well — isn't obvious. AI Resource Hub was built to fix that: a single, trustworthy place to find good AI tools and learn how to use them effectively, without wading through hype or ads.

What you'll find here

Right now, the site offers a curated directory of AI tools across chatbots, image generation, video generation, and writing/productivity, along with a free Prompt Generator to help you build clear, effective prompts for text, image, and video AI — no experience required.

Where this is headed

AI Resource Hub is just getting started. Over time it will grow into a fuller AI service platform, including more advanced tools, deeper guides, and a subscriber tier for premium features. For now, everything on the site is free to use.
